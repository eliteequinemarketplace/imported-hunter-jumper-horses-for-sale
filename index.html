Elite Equine Marketplace features imported hunter jumper horses from Europe — connecting US buyers directly with trusted import agents, European sellers, and professional riders bringing quality sport horses to the American market. European warmbloods have long been the gold standard in the hunter jumper world, prized for their natural movement, scope, trainability, and the quality of their breeding programs.

Why Buy a European Import for Hunter Jumper Sport?

European warmblood breeding programs — particularly in the Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, and France — have spent decades selectively breeding horses for the specific qualities that hunter jumper sport demands: natural, flowing movement, a balanced and adjustable canter, a careful and scopey jump, and a trainable, rideable temperament. Many of the top hunter, jumper, equitation, and derby horses competing in North America today are European imports or have significant European warmblood bloodlines.

Importing a horse from Europe can offer exceptional value — horses that might cost significantly more in the US market are often available directly from European sellers at competitive prices, particularly young horses and developing prospects that have not yet been discovered by the US market.

KWPN (Dutch Warmblood) Horses for Sale in the USA

The KWPN — Koninklijk Warmbloed Paardenstamboek Nederland, the Royal Dutch Warmblood Studbook — produces some of the most sought-after hunter jumper horses in the world. Known for their exceptional movement, natural scope, and trainable temperament, KWPN horses excel in every hunter jumper discipline from hunters through Grand Prix jumping. KWPN hunters are particularly prized for their naturally flat, flowing jump and ground-covering trot — qualities that win consistently in the hunter ring. Elite Equine Marketplace lists KWPN horses imported from the Netherlands, with full KWPN registration, inspection scores, and competition records where applicable.

Belgian Warmblood (BWP) Horses for Sale in the USA

Belgian Warmblood horses from the BWP studbook are renowned for their scope, carefulness, and natural jumping ability — making them a top choice for jumper riders at every level. BWP horses have produced Olympic and World Championship jumpers and are consistently represented at the highest levels of the sport. Belgian Warmbloods imported to the US often come with documented CSI competition records and FEI results, making them ideal for serious jumper programs and ambitious amateur and junior riders.

Hanoverian Horses for Sale in the USA

Hanoverian horses from Germany are among the most versatile and popular warmblood imports in the US hunter jumper market. Known for their elegant movement, correct conformation, and reliable temperament, Hanoverians excel in both the hunter and equitation rings — particularly prized for their naturally correct and expressive trot and canter. Hanoverian imports with Hannoveraner Verband registration and inspection documentation are highly sought after by US hunter and equitation programs.

Other European Breeds — Oldenburg, Selle Francais, Irish Sport Horse

Beyond KWPN, BWP, and Hanoverian, Elite Equine Marketplace lists hunter jumper imports from a range of European studbooks. Oldenburg horses from Germany are known for their scope and versatility. Selle Francais horses from France have produced world-class show jumpers with exceptional athleticism and bravery. Irish Sport Horses combine the Thoroughbred's speed and heart with warmblood scope and rideability — making them a popular choice for jumper and derby riders.

What to Look for When Buying a European Import

When purchasing a European import, key considerations include studbook registration and inspection scores, CSI or national competition records, veterinary history and pre-purchase examination results, shipper and import agent reputation, and quarantine and importation documentation. Elite Equine Marketplace listings for European imports include as much of this information as sellers provide, and buyers are always encouraged to work with a trusted import agent and veterinarian when purchasing a horse from abroad.
